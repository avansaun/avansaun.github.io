About EmotionAll

EmotionAll gives you a general idea of peoples' mood around the world based on Twitter post content. It uses data provided by wefeel.csiro.au, which analyzes key words in tweets that show emotion. (It's a pretty cool site, you should check it out)

This website was inspired by this Instructable.

Also, please note that this site's content is purely for entertainment purposes, and is not intended to be an accurate assessment of any geographic area.
