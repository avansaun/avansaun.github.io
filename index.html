A Little About Us...

Freeport Travel Planning is an up and coming business dedicated to providing our customers with trips that will make lifetime memories. We also believe that as many people as possible should be able to experience the world around them, so we work with transportation, lodging, and recreational companies to offer limited-time deals to you. When you schedule a meeting with one of our friendly staff members, you'll get to choose from handpicked resorts and itenaries, so you know your trip will be amazing.

We also put quality ahead of everything else we do, because our customers drive our business. When you book your trip through us, you gain peace of mind with our extensive money-back guarantee, with refunds offered up to the very day of the planned start of your trip. And once you initially book your trip, all of our knowledgeable staff are just a phone call away, free of charge.
