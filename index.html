We put the POWER
in your hands

It's your getaway, and you deserve to have full control over all the details. Our job at Freeport Travel is to empower you to make them happen. We work tirelessly for our customers to uncover an extensive selection of destinations, escursions, transportation, and boarding options, wherever you want to go.

Take uncertainty out of the equation

Relaxation. Peace of mind. Rest. We're guessing that you want these when you travel. We do too, which is why we structured our business model around eliminating risk to you, our customers. When you book with us, you can have complete assurance that your trip will go on. We intervene with hotels, transportation, and excursion companies on your behalf if you aren't satisfied with them, and will do our best to make things smooth.

Your wallet will thank you too

Why spend more than you need to? We partner with a wide selection of companies in the transportation, hotel, and recreation industries to pass on the deals we get straight to you, which means you could save up to 40% on your next trip.
